NEUROPLASTICITY

Neuroplasticity is the brain's ability to change and adapt as a result of experience. Our brains are truly extraordinary, unlike computers, which always get a software update and have the same hardware, our brains can change the hardware as well. Different pathways are created and are discarded, according to our experiences. By stimulating our brain learning something new we create new connections between our neurons and actually change the brain's physical appearance.more...
